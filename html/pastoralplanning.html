2020 Diocesan Pastoral Plan

The Diocese of Steubenville is facing a priest crisis. 75 years ago, when this Diocese was formed, there were 150 active priests. Today there are only 38. Of those 38, only 10 are under the age of 60. Some difficult decisions will have to be made. Some parishes will have to be merged and some parishes will need to be closed.


Bishop Monforton has called all of the priests of the Diocese, along with representative parishioners, to form a plan to deal with the priest shortage. The following documents were sent out in preparation for these meetings.
